About Us

The Vizag We Care (VWC) program was formed by a team of like minded individuals from all over the world, who have come together in solidarity to help the minority community in Vizag affected by the COVID-19 pandemic. Our roots in this town go back a long way and this pandemic has brought us closer to our community than ever before. As a priority, we aim to support people most at risk in the community. It is crucial that individuals affected by the virus, including those that have been isolated and lack resources, have access to food, medicine and essential services to help them in their recovery. Funds raised will go towards ongoing support to the community. Your contribution to this great cause will enable us to fulfil our mission. We sincerely ask you to pledge your support by donating to the Go Fund Me account set up for the VWC program - you can access the link below and make a donation - no amount is too big or too small and we thank you in advance for your generosity

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can we request for Financial aid?

    We only provide food packages and medicines . If you need anything else please contact us via email and we will see if it can be fulfilled.

  • Will you be able to help me get a job?

    Please fill i the form requesting for help to find a job and we will try to help you.

  • If I have a job , can I still get a package?

    The packages are for those who do not have a job and have no means of procuring food.
